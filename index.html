About Us

Est. 2009 · New York City

Maison Élara was born from a simple belief — that a great meal is never just about the food. Nestled in the heart of Manhattan, our kitchen draws on classical French technique and the finest seasonal ingredients to craft dishes that are as considered as they are delicious. Every plate that leaves our kitchen tells a story.

Our head chef, Olivier Renaud, trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens in Lyon before bringing his vision to New York. He works directly with local farms and artisan producers to ensure that what arrives on your table is honest, seasonal, and extraordinary.

"We don't just cook food. We compose experiences." — Olivier Renaud, Head Chef
